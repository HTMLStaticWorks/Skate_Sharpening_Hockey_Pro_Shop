TEAM GEAR | AUGUST 2026

MODERN TEAM JERSEY TRENDS: FROM VINTAGE TO NEON

The look of a hockey team is just as important as the way they play. This season, we're seeing a massive shift in jersey design trends, blending classic aesthetics with futuristic technology.

The Rise of 'Faux-Backs'

Vintage is in. Many teams are opting for "faux-back" designs—brand new jerseys that use cream-colored whites, heavy stripes, and retro typography to create a sense of history, even for brand new clubs.

Pro Tip:

"When designing a jersey, less is often more. A simple, bold logo and three consistent colors will always look better on the ice than a complex, multi-colored mural."

Neon & Hi-Vis Accents

On the other end of the spectrum, we're seeing a surge in neon and high-visibility accents. Think "Electric Blue" or "Atomic Green" piping on a matte black jersey. These designs are popular among youth teams and beer league clubs looking to stand out under the arena lights.

Planning your team's new look for next season? IcePro's custom design team is here to help you build a jersey that your players will be proud to wear.
