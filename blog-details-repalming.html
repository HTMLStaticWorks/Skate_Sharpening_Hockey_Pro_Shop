GLOVE REPALMING: DON'T THROW AWAY YOUR FAVORITE GLOVES

We've all been there. Your favorite gloves are perfectly broken in, but the palms are starting to look like swiss cheese. Most players think it's time to buy a new pair, but there's a better way: Repalming.

The Benefits of Repalming

Repalming allows you to keep the protective outer shell and the "broken-in" feel of your gloves while giving you a brand new grip surface. It's often cheaper than buying a mid-range pair of gloves and allows you to choose high-end materials like Nash or Clarino leather.

Pro Tip:

"Check your palms for 'crusting'. This is a sign that the sweat and moisture have dried out the leather, making it brittle. Regular washing and air-drying can prevent this!"

When is it too late?

If the protective foam on the back of the hand or fingers is starting to break down, or if the plastic inserts are cracked, then it's time to retire the gloves. However, if the only issue is the palm, a professional repalming at IcePro can make them feel like new again.

Bring your gloves into IcePro today for a free inspection. We offer a variety of palm materials and custom stitching options.
