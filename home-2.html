Pro Shop FAQ

How often should I sharpen my skates?
Most active players sharpen every 10-15 hours of ice time. However, it depends on your weight, skating style, and ice hardness.
What is the difference between manual and automated sharpening?
Automated sharpening (like Sparx) provides unmatched consistency, while manual sharpening allows for highly specific custom profiles. We offer both to match your preference.
Do you offer same-day equipment repair?
Small repairs like rivet replacement or minor stitching can often be done while you wait. Major repairs typically require 2-3 business days.
Can you handle bulk team jersey orders?
Yes! We specialize in team apparel and offer significant discounts for bulk orders of 12 units or more.
