THE ART OF THE PERFECT TAPE JOB

Every player has their ritual. For many, it starts with the tape. Whether you're a toe-to-heel or heel-to-toe kind of player, the way you tape your stick can significantly impact your puck feel and shot accuracy.

Why Taping Matters

Tape provides the friction necessary to grip the puck. Without it, the puck would slide right off your blade. Moreover, tape protects the composite blade from the harsh impacts of pucks and the ice, extending its lifespan.

Pro Tip:

"Always use a puck to smooth out the tape after you're done. This removes air bubbles and creates a tighter bond with the blade."

Top 3 Taping Styles

  • The Classic: Full blade coverage from heel to toe. Maximum protection and consistent feel.
  • The Toe-Only: Popular among elite snipers who want maximum puck feel at the sweet spot of the blade.
  • The Strip: A single strip along the bottom followed by a standard wrap. Extra protection for the high-wear area.
