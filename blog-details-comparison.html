BAUER VAPOR VS CCM RIBCOR: THE ULTIMATE SHOWDOWN

The two giants of the hockey world have released their latest flagship skates. The Bauer Vapor HyperLite 2 and the CCM Ribcor 100K are designed for maximum agility, but they take very different approaches to get there.

The Bauer Vapor Experience

Bauer's Vapor line has always been about explosive acceleration and a quick, tight fit. The HyperLite 2 continues this tradition with a stiffer quarter package and the new Powerfly holder, which is designed to increase energy transfer during the push-off phase.

Pro Tip:

"If you're a player who relies on pure straight-line speed and explosive starts, the Vapor is hard to beat. If you prioritize comfort and lateral agility, the Ribcor might be your winner."

The CCM Ribcor Experience

CCM's Ribcor is the only "flexible" high-end skate on the market. It's designed for players who want a more natural range of motion. The Ribcor 100K features a unique one-piece boot that wraps around the foot like a second skin, providing unparalleled comfort and "feel" for the ice.

Still can't decide? Come into IcePro and try them both on our synthetic ice testing zone. Our fit experts will ensure you leave with the perfect boot for your playing style.
